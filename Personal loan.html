What are the different types of personal loans?

1.HOME IMPROVEMENT LOAN

For most people, your home is your largest asset, so you want to keep it in good working order and as updated as possible to protect your return on investment. Home improvements and repairs can be pricey though, which is why some homeowners can visit 3rd Time Loans and seek out home improvement loans.

2.WEDDING LOAN

For couples who are unable to pay significant wedding costs out of pocket like hiring a tent, buying a wedding gown, and maybe for bride price(lobola), a wedding loan can be one financing option as 3rd Time Loans can get covered in less time.

3.Medical loan

Medical expenses can quickly add up, and if you’re unable to pay, you may consider a medical loan to cover them or to take care of living expenses while you recover.

3rd Time Loans gives the medical loans to customers which allows them to pay for their medical fees like when doing some operations etc.

4.EDUCATIONAL LOAN

An education loan is a sum of money borrowed to finance post-secondary education or higher education-related expenses. Education loans are intended to cover the cost of tuition, books and supplies, and living expenses while the borrower is in the process of pursuing a degree. So 3rd Time Loans is also giving the educational loan.

5.VACATION/TRAVEL LOAN

A vacation is an unsecured personal loan you can use to pay for travel expenses, inclucding flights, hotels, rental cars, meals or anything else trip-related.

These loans don't require collateral, and you reapay them in fixed monthly installments, meaning your monthly salary won't change over the course of the loan.
